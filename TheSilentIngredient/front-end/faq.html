FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ's)


GENERAL ENQUIRIES

What is TheSilentIngredient?
TheSilentIngredient consist in a web based ingredient recipe search engine that provides recipes based on the spare ingredients you have at home, but have no idea what to cook with it.
Why should anyone use TheSilentIngredient?
The website allows you to pick from a variety of diets, also allowing you to select an option to only have healthy recipes with calorie intake details based on your diet selected. This provides you with a learning and helpful experience by trying recipes you may have never tried before. It also encourages you to cook more and eat less pre-cooked/fast food (saves money) and stay healthier.
What UN sustainable goals is TheSilentIngredient focusing on?
TheSilentIngredient falls under more than one of the following goals:

  • No Poverty
  • Zero Hunger
  • Climate action
  • Reduce inequalities
  • Life on land & below water
  • Good Health and well being
  • Responsible consumption and production

    How can ingredients be selected/added?
    Ingredients can be added in the Recipe's search sidebar by clicking on the same and typing the desired ingredient.
    How can ingredients be removed/deleted?
    Ingredients can be removed/deleted from your list one by one by clicking on the (X) button. As you may have experienced, once added the ingredients stack themselves, making it easy to delete them as well.
    How can I remove all ingredients?
    In the sidebar, a "Clear" button is presented that allows you to remove/delete the entire selected list of ingredients. By clicking on it you are emptying the ingredient list as well as the entire recipe search results.

    DIETS / RECIPES FILTERING

    What types of diets exist and how can they be selected?
    TheSilentIngredient supports around 5 diets including:

  • Vegan
  • Vegetarian
  • Halal
  • Lactose Free
  • Gluten Free

    • These diets can be selected by pressing the dropdown list (-Diet-) allowing you to select your type of diet!
    What is the purpose of the "Healthy Recipes" checkbox?
    The checkbox is a personal recipes advice and suggestion from TheSilentIngredient to let you know what recipes we consider to be healthy and that may eye catch your interest. Again, all recipes displayed when clicked, are mere suggestions from our team!
    How can one diet be unselected?
    After selecting a specific diet, in case you wish to go back to general recipes, you only need to click on the dropdown list option called "-Diet-" and all recipes displayed will not have any specific diet impact as before.

What is TheSilentIngredient?
TheSilentIngredient consist in a web based ingredient recipe search engine that provides recipes based on the spare ingredients you have at home, but have no idea what to cook with it.
Why should anyone use TheSilentIngredient?
The website allows you to pick from a variety of diets, also allowing you to select an option to only have healthy recipes with calorie intake details based on your diet selected. This provides you with a learning and helpful experience by trying recipes you may have never tried before. It also encourages you to cook more and eat less pre-cooked/fast food (saves money) and stay healthier.
What UN sustainable goals is TheSilentIngredient focusing on?
TheSilentIngredient falls under more than one of the following goals:

  • No Poverty
  • Zero Hunger
  • Climate action
  • Reduce inequalities
  • Life on land & below water
  • Good Health and well being
  • Responsible consumption and production
